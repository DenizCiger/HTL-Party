How to play:

The concept is simple:

  1. Roll the dice
  2. Your character moves as many fields as you rolled
  3. After everyone did their turn, a random Minigame starts
  4. If you perform better in the Minigame, your ability will be charged more
  5. Use these Items to either slow down your oppenents, or to protect yourself from them
  6. First person that gets to the finish line, wins!

Currently these Items are available:

Texture Name Description
Energy Shield Protects the user from getting hit for 1 round
Rocket Shoots at a field, causing all players that stand on it to take damage
