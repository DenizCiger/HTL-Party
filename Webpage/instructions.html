How to play:

The concept is simple:

  1. Choose a character
  2. Attack other players to damage them
  3. When you hit a player, they get knocked back
  4. The more damage someone took, the more knockback they get
  5. If you are knocked off a stage, you lose a life
  6. Last Person with lifes wins!
